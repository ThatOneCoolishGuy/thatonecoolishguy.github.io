HTML-Crafted.

The Javascript-free mark for 90's styled websites.

What is HTML-Crafted?

HTML-Crafted is a mark that you have the absolute freedom to put on your website. It lets the viewers of your site know, that there is no Javascript in your website. It lets them know, that your site is strictly HTML and CSS.

What is the purpose of HTML-Crafted?

The sole purpose of HTML-Crafted is to bring together the web1 community, with JS-less websites. I personally love Javascript, but I truely believe it is overused.
